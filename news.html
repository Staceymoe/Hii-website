July 15, 2026

Hii begins building the public World Model

A dedicated public foundation is created for a human-reviewed systems map showing active themes, evidence labels, the publication gate, and the planned progression from hourly scanning to selective public updates and visible revision history.

Open project

Research integrity architecture expands

Hii completes a provisional Research Audit v0.2 package and adopts a research operating system covering claim maturity, verification, provenance, correction, model handoffs, publication controls, and human accountability.

Research overview

Hii launches its first public website

The first public front door opens at hii.earth with the Earth identity, six research areas, early project descriptions, and the institutional positioning of Hii as a bridge between emerging AI evidence and real-world human adaptation.

About Hii
June 21, 2026

Human-AI co-adaptive intelligence proposal submitted to AGI-26

Hii submits a conference proposal based on an 18-month longitudinal human-AI collaboration, centered on shared representations, recursive validation, complementarity, rupture and repair, and relationship as the unit of analysis.

An eight-module working curriculum is assembled to help clinicians understand AI-mediated emotional life, situational overwhelm, relational patterns, stabilization, risk distinctions, and emerging clinical language.

Boundary

News should record progress, not manufacture momentum.

Hii will not present internal activity as external validation. A completed document is not the same as a completed pilot. A submission is not an acceptance. A coherent concept is not a proven product. Public updates will preserve those distinctions.
