Emerging Product

The Hii Observatory turns orientation into a usable instrument.

The Observatory concept brings together the World Model, evidence labels, relational AI literacy, research updates, clinician learning, revision history, and role-specific pathways. It remains pre-validation. Hii will test a bounded demonstration and guided pilot before treating recurring software as the primary business model.

Project Rules

Build only what helps a real person orient, decide, or act.

Bounded

Start narrow

Each project begins with a defined audience, problem, evidence boundary, and human review gate.

Honest

Show maturity

Active, pilot, in development, submitted, published, and externally validated are visibly different states.

Relational

Protect agency

Tools should increase human judgment, connection, consent, and capacity rather than replace them.
